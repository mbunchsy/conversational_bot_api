Return Policy:
Products purchased from our store can be returned within a maximum of 30 calendar days from the delivery date. The product must be in its original condition and show no signs of use. Customized items are not eligible for return.

Refund Policy:
Once the return has been received and verified, the refund will be processed within a maximum of 10 business days. Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original purchase.

Shipping Policy:
Orders are processed within 24 to 48 hours and delivered within an estimated 2 to 5 business days for domestic shipments. International deliveries may take between 7 and 15 days. No deliveries are made on weekends or public holidays.

Privacy Policy:
All personal information collected is used solely for order processing and service improvement. We do not share data with third parties without the user's prior consent.

Exchange Policy:
Product exchanges are allowed within the same 30-day period and are subject to stock availability. The customer must cover the shipping costs associated with the exchange, unless the exchange is due to a company error.

Customer Support:
Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local time). You can reach us via email or through the live chat on our website.

Cancellation Policy:
Orders can be canceled as long as they have not been shipped. Once shipped, the customer must wait to receive the order and then request a return if necessary.
